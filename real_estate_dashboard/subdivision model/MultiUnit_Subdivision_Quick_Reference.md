## üí° THE 5 EXIT STRATEGIES

### Strategy 1: SUBDIVIDE/CONVERT & SELL
**Timeline:** 12-18 months | **ROI Target:** 500-1,500%  
**Best For:** Maximum profit, strong condo market  
**Requires:** County approval, 12+ month timeline, conversion capital

### Strategy 2: SELL AS-IS  
**Timeline:** 3-9 months | **ROI Target:** 200-400%  
**Best For:** Quick exit, limited capital  
**Requires:** Multi-unit investor comps

### Strategy 3: BRRRR (Refinance & Hold)
**Timeline:** 18-24 months | **Return:** 8-15% COC + appreciation  
**Best For:** Portfolio builders, long-term hold  
**Requires:** 20% down, refinance to 125%+ of cost

### Strategy 4: HYBRID (Sell Some, Hold Some)
**Timeline:** 12-15 months | **ROI Target:** 500-1,000% + cash flow  
**Best For:** Best of both worlds  
**Requires:** Conversion approval, mixed strategy

### Strategy 5: WHOLESALE (Assignment)
**Timeline:** 30-90 days | **Fee:** $20K-$50K  
**Best For:** No money deals, quick cash  
**Requires:** Buyer network, off-market deal

## üìä KEY METRICS & TARGETS

### Conversion Premium Test
```
Premium = (Individual Unit Sales - As-Is Value) √∑ As-Is Value

‚úÖ 25-35%: Good opportunity
‚úÖ 35-50%: Excellent opportunity
‚ùå <15%: Don't convert, sell as-is
```

### The 70% Rule (Multi-Unit)
```
Max Purchase = (ARV √ó 70%) - Renovation - Conversion

Example:
3 units √ó $370K = $1,110,000
70% = $777,000
Less reno: $62,700
Less conversion: $34,650
MAX OFFER: $679,650
```

### Cost Per Unit Guidelines

**Renovation:**
- Light: $5K-$15K per unit
- Moderate: $15K-$30K per unit  
- Heavy: $30K-$50K+ per unit

**Conversion:**
- Duplex: $15K-$30K total
- Triplex: $20K-$40K total
- Quadplex: $25K-$50K total
- 5-10 units: $50K-$100K+ total

### Reserve Capital Formula
```
Reserve = Monthly Carry √ó 12 months

Monthly Carry = Mortgage + Taxes + Insurance + Utilities + Maintenance

Example: $6,000/month √ó 12 = $72,000 reserve
```

## ‚ö° CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### ‚úÖ DO THESE FIRST

1. **Call County Planning Department**
   - "Are condo conversions allowed at [address]?"
   - Get approval timeline
   - Understand fee structure
   - Get answer in writing

2. **Get 3 Contractor Bids**
   - Walk property with licensed contractors
   - Itemized bids by unit
   - Use HIGHEST bid (not average)
   - Add 15-20% contingency

3. **Pull Comps**
   - 5-10 recent condo sales (similar area/size)
   - 3-5 multi-unit sales (same property type)
   - Calculate $/SF for your units
   - Verify conversion premium

4. **Get Attorney Quotes**
   - Interview 2-3 attorneys
   - Specialize in conversions
   - Typical cost: $10K-$15K
   - Ask about success rate

5. **Secure Financing**
   - Get pre-approved
   - Know your maximum leverage
   - Have backup lender
   - Understand LTV requirements

## üéì COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Not Checking Zoning First**
   - Call county BEFORE making offer
   - Get approval in writing
   - Make offer contingent

2. **Underestimating Timeline**
   - Always double time estimates
   - Plan for delays
   - Have adequate reserves

3. **Using Estimated Renovation Costs**
   - Get actual contractor bids
   - Use highest bid + 15%
   - Budget for surprises

4. **Forgetting Conversion Costs**
   - Budget $20K-$50K for legal/conversion
   - Get attorney quote upfront
   - Include in total project analysis

5. **Overpaying on Purchase**
   - Use 70% Rule formula
   - Never fall in love with property
   - Walk away from bad deals

6. **Insufficient Reserves**
   - Need 6-12 months carry costs
   - Plan for extended timeline
   - Have backup capital

7. **Poor Market Timing**
   - List condos in spring/fall
   - Avoid winter months
   - Time renovation accordingly
