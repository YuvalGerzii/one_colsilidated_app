### The Subdivision Premium

**Why do individual condos sell for more than the duplex?**

1. **Buyer Pool:**
   - Duplex buyers: Investors (5% of market)
   - Condo buyers: Owner-occupants (95% of market)
   - **Result:** More competition = higher prices

2. **Financing:**
   - Duplex: Investment property rates (higher)
   - Condo: Primary residence rates (lower)
   - **Result:** Buyers can afford more

3. **Perception:**
   - Duplex: "Investment/rental"
   - Condo: "My home"
   - **Result:** Emotional premium

4. **The Math:**
   - Duplex as-is: $850,000 (cap rate driven)
   - 2 Condos: $450K each = $900,000
   - **Premium:** $50,000 (5.9%)
   - Less subdivision costs: $27,000
   - **Net gain:** $23,000

**Is it worth it?**
- Extra profit: $23,000+
- Extra time: 3-6 months
- Extra complexity: Moderate
- **Answer:** Usually YES in strong markets

## üéì PRO TIPS

### 1. Talk to County FIRST

**Before doing anything:**
- Call Miami-Dade Planning Department
- Ask about condo conversion for THIS ADDRESS
- Specific questions:
  - "Is this parcel eligible for condo conversion?"
  - "What's the typical timeline?"
  - "Any special requirements?"
  - "What's the fee schedule?"

**Red Flags:**
- "Not allowed in this zone"
- "Moratorium on conversions"
- "18+ month timeline"
- "Fees over $10,000"

### 2. Negotiate the Price

**Your target:** $700,000 (not $735,000)

**Why?**
- Listed 230 days (stale listing)
- Price increased $10K (seller testing market)
- Can show occupied (not vacant)

**Offer strategy:**
- Start at $690,000
- Justify: "Based on rent vs market cap rate"
- Point to long days on market
- Every $10K saved = 45% ROI improvement

### 3. Consider NOT Renovating

**Option:** Skip renovations entirely

**Why?**
- Property recently renovated
- Brand new roof
- Buyers will personalize anyway
- Saves $42,000

**Adjust model:**
- Remove renovation costs
- Reduce timeline by 3 months
- Sell as "renovated duplex" or "move-in ready condos"
- **Result:** Even higher ROI

### 4. Hybrid Strategy

**Consider:** Subdivide but don't sell immediately

**Process:**
1. Buy property ($735K, 3% down)
2. Complete condo conversion (6 months)
3. Increase rents to market ($5,300/month)
4. Hold for 1-2 years as rental
5. Sell when market peaks

**Benefits:**
- Cash flow while waiting
- Tax benefits (depreciation)
- Market timing flexibility
- Lower capital gains (held 1+ year)

### 5. FHA Loan Alternative

**If 3% conventional doesn't work:**

Consider FHA 3.5% down:
- Owner-occupy one unit
- Rent the other
- Loan limits: Up to $1,149,825 for duplex in Miami
- Lower rate than investor loan
- **Catch:** Must live there 1 year

**Pros:**
- Lower rate (saves money)
- Easier approval
- Lower down payment

**Cons:**
- Must owner-occupy 1 year
- Delays subdivision timeline
- PMI costs
