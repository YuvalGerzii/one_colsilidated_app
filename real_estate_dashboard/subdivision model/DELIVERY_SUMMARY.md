### Exit Strategies Analyzed

**1. Subdivide/Convert & Sell Individually**
- Timeline: 12-18 months
- ROI Target: 500-1,500%
- Best For: Maximum profit, strong condo markets
- Process: Legal conversion ‚Üí renovation ‚Üí individual unit sales

**2. Sell As-Is (Multi-Unit)**
- Timeline: 3-9 months
- ROI Target: 200-400%
- Best For: Quick exit, limited capital
- Process: Purchase ‚Üí optional light renovation ‚Üí sell to investors

**3. BRRRR (Buy, Renovate, Rent, Refinance, Repeat)**
- Timeline: 18-24 months
- Return: 8-15% COC + appreciation
- Best For: Portfolio builders, long-term hold
- Process: Purchase ‚Üí renovate ‚Üí stabilize ‚Üí refinance ‚Üí hold

**4. Hybrid (Sell Some, Hold Some)**
- Timeline: 12-15 months
- ROI Target: 500-1,000% + cash flow
- Best For: Best of both worlds
- Process: Convert ‚Üí sell some units ‚Üí refinance ‚Üí hold remainder

**5. Wholesale (Assignment)**
- Timeline: 30-90 days
- Fee: $20K-$50K
- Best For: No money deals, quick cash
- Process: Contract ‚Üí market to investors ‚Üí assign for fee

## üö® CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### The 4 Pillars of Success

**1. BUY RIGHT (70% Rule)**
- Purchase below 70% of ARV
- Less renovation costs
- Less conversion costs
- = Maximum purchase price

**2. VERIFY ZONING**
- County allows conversion
- Get approval timeline in writing
- Understand all costs
- Check for moratoriums

**3. GET ACTUAL BIDS**
- Walk property with 3 contractors
- Get detailed itemized bids
- Use HIGHEST bid for budget
- Add 15-20% contingency

**4. HAVE RESERVES**
- Budget 12 months carry costs
- Double all timeline estimates
- Plan for delays and surprises
- Have backup capital sources
