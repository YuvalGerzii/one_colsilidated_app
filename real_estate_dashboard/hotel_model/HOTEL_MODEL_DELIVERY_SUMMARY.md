## ðŸ”‘ KEY METRICS EXPLAINED

### Operating Metrics

**ADR (Average Daily Rate)**
- Formula: Total Room Revenue / Rooms Sold
- 2024 US Average: $159
- Key driver of revenue

**Occupancy Rate**
- Formula: Rooms Sold / Rooms Available
- 2024 US Average: 63.0%
- Measures utilization

**RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room)**
- Formula: ADR Ã— Occupancy
- 2024 US Average: $100
- Most important operating metric

**GOP (Gross Operating Profit)**
- Formula: Total Revenue - Operating Expenses
- Used for hotel valuation
- Target margins: 25-45% depending on segment

### Investment Metrics

**Yield on Cost**
- Formula: Stabilized NOI / Total Development Cost
- Target: Exit cap + 100-200 bps
- Measures development profit

**Levered IRR**
- Time-adjusted return on equity
- Target: 15-25% depending on strategy
- Accounts for timing of cash flows

**Equity Multiple**
- Formula: Total Cash Returned / Equity Invested
- Target: 2.0-3.0x over 7-10 years
- Simple profit measure

## ðŸŽ“ MODEL METHODOLOGY

### Revenue Projections
1. **Year 1**: Ramp-up period reduces occupancy
2. **Year 2+**: Stabilized occupancy achieved
3. **ADR**: Grows at annual growth rate (2.5-4.0% typical)
4. **F&B**: Outlet revenue + banquet/catering for group business
5. **Other**: Meeting space, parking, spa, other ancillary

### Expense Structure
1. **Department Expenses**: Variable with revenue (rooms, F&B, other)
2. **Undistributed Expenses**: Semi-fixed per room (A&G, POM, utilities)
3. **Fixed Charges**: Franchise fees, insurance, property tax

### Cash Flow Analysis
1. **Operating Cash Flow**: NOI from operations
2. **Debt Service**: Annual loan payment (PMT function)
3. **FF&E Reserve**: 4% of revenue for renovations
4. **Net Cash Flow**: Cash available to equity investors

### Return Calculation
1. **Exit Value**: Year 10 NOI / Exit Cap Rate
2. **Net Proceeds**: Value - Selling Costs - Loan Balance
3. **IRR**: Internal rate of return on equity cash flows
4. **Equity Multiple**: Total return / Initial equity
